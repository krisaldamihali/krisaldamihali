## 🧠 About Me

I am passionate about **software engineering and artificial intelligence**, focused on building intelligent systems and impactful digital solutions. Aiming to apply skills across AI, development, and modern technologies to create innovative and scalable products. Driven by continuous learning, collaboration, and contributing to meaningful advancements in technology.

- 🤖 **AI & Deep Learning:** Expertise in Neural Networks, training pipelines, diagnostics, and innovative AI modeling.
- 🏗️ **Software Architecture:** Designing robust backends and interactive, high-end frontend experiences.
- 🖥️ **Full-Stack Development:** Expertise in React, Node.js, Flask, Spring Boot, and many more.
- 💬 **Accessibility:** Advanced Text-to-Speech (TTS) and Speech-to-Text (STT) integration for inclusive design.
- 🔬 **Research & Innovation:** Bridging research and production in AI, Cybersecurity, data storytelling, and beyond.
